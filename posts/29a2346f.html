Overview of AI in Finance

前言

金融人工智能小作业记录。从多个角度阐释了下面几个问题：

  1. What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?
  2. What is Computational Finance?
  3. How does computation impact on classical economics?
  4. How could a trader take advantage of AI research?

原文

AI is the Intelligence displayed by machines. It simulates human behaviour or thinking and can be trained to solve specific problems.
AI models are usually trained using ‘Big data’, and they have the ability to make intelligent decisions.

Computational Finance is a branch of applied computer science that deals with problems of practical interest in finance. In another word, it exploits synergy by applying advanced computing to finance & economics. As AI developed, computational finance came to rely on algorithms and ‘Big Data’.

CS perspective computation is challenging classical finance. In the classical economics theory, there are 3 assumptions that not very practical. If these assumptions hold, much of computer science & artificial intelligence is not worth studying or irrelevant.
On one hand, classical economics take computation for granted, not even an issue. On the other, computation in CS perspective computation is vital but very limited, which has more ‘Intractable’ problems, and sacrifices the optimality and completeness. From this perspective, they complement each other’s deficiencies and the computation is more of a powerful tool for perfecting and correcting classical finance.

For traders, finding the hidden opportunity is the target. The computers are fast and useful to lend some help. For traders themselves, they need more knowledge besides finance and economics. To beat competitors, traders also need good knowledge of algorithms. Some algorithms & heuristics can help traders to find better solutions while some are faster than others. The factors ignored by classical economics usually need to be considered.

机翻

AI是机器显示的智能。它模拟人类的行为或思维，可以训练解决特定问题。
AI模型通常使用”大数据”进行训练，并且具有做出智能决策的能力。

计算金融学是应用计算机科学的一个分支，处理金融学中实际感兴趣的问题。换句话说，它通过将先进的计算应用于金融和经济来利用协同作用。随着人工智能的发展，计算金融开始依赖算法和”大数据”

CS视角计算正在挑战经典金融。在古典经济学理论中，有3个不是很实用的假设。如果这些假设成立，那么计算机科学和人工智能的大部分内容就不值得研究或无关紧要。
一方面，古典经济学认为计算是理所当然的，甚至不是一个问题。另一方面，CS计算视角下的计算至关重要，它的计算能力十分有限，会出现很多的”棘手”问题，并牺牲了最优性和完整性。从这个角度来看，它们相互补充了彼此的不足，计算更像是完善和纠正经典金融的有力工具。计算机速度快，很有用，可以提供一些帮助。

对于交易者本身来说，除了金融和经济学之外，他们还需要更多的知识。为了击败竞争对手，交易者还需要对算法有很好的了解。一些算法和启发式算法可以帮助交易者找到更好的解决方案，而有些算法和启发式算法比其他算法更快。古典经济学所忽略的因素通常需要考虑。
